About Us



The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) is an autonomous organization under the Ministry of Education, Government of India. Established in 1961, its primary objective is to improve the quality of school education across the country. Here are some key points about NCERT:

  1. Curriculum Development: NCERT plays a crucial role in developing and revising the national curriculum framework for school education. It designs textbooks, syllabi, and teaching materials for various subjects, ensuring uniformity and standardization.
  2. Research and Innovation: NCERT conducts research in education, pedagogy, and curriculum development. It explores innovative teaching methods, assessment techniques, and educational technologies to enhance learning outcomes.
  3. Teacher Training and Capacity Building: NCERT organizes workshops, training programs, and orientation sessions for teachers. It aims to enhance their teaching skills, update their knowledge, and promote effective classroom practices.
  4. Textbook Publication: NCERT publishes textbooks for classes I to XII across different subjects. These textbooks are widely used in schools affiliated with various education boards in India.
  5. Educational Resources: Apart from textbooks, NCERT provides supplementary materials, digital resources, and educational kits to support teachers and students. It also develops e-learning platforms and mobile apps.
  6. Promotion of Inclusive Education: NCERT focuses on inclusive education by creating content that caters to diverse learners, including those with special needs. It promotes equity, access, and quality education for all.
  7. Research Journals and Publications: NCERT publishes research journals, books, and monographs related to education. These publications contribute to the academic discourse and inform educational policies.
